Reset local test data

Use this before end-to-end testing. It clears the shared upressease_db bucket, sign-in sessions, cart, orders cache, and related keys. Your theme choice (upress-theme) is kept.

After a reset, the app will not auto-load demo users or transactions until you click “Restore demo dataset” or remove the key upressease_skip_demo from DevTools.

You can also run in the console on any page that loads storage.js: resetUpresseaseLocalStorage() · restoreUpresseaseDemoDatabase()

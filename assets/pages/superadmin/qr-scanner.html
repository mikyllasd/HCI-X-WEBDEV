How to Use QR Scanner

  1. Ask the student to show their order QR code from the confirmation page.
  2. Use the camera scanner above or manually enter the Order ID.
  3. Verify the order details and payment status.
  4. If payment is complete and order is ready, click "Release Order".
  5. Hand over the order to the student and mark it as completed.

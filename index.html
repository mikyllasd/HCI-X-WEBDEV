2. User Verification

All users are required to upload a Validated Student ID and 1st Semester COR every after one year. UPRESSease reserves the right to reject accounts with falsified documents.

3. Customization and Copyright

You are solely responsible for the designs, logos, and images you upload for printing. UPRESSease reserves the right to refuse printing any material that is deemed offensive or violates copyright laws.

4. Payment and Production

All orders are final once production begins. For GCash payments, production will only start once the proof of payment is verified.

5. Claiming of Orders

Users will be notified via SMS or through the portal once their order is ready. Unclaimed orders after 30 days may be disposed of, and no refunds will be issued for customized items.

How It Works

Simple Steps to
Get Your Print

Our streamlined process makes ordering quick and hassle-free.

1

Create Account

Register with your WMSU email and upload your Certificate of Registration (COR) for verification.

2

Upload & Customize

Upload your files and select your preferences - paper size, color, binding type, and more.

3

Pay & Confirm

Choose your payment method - GCash, Cash, or Cash on Pickup. Receive order confirmation.

4

Pick Up

Get notified when your order is ready. Show your QR code at the counter for quick pickup.
